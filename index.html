Connect & Explore.

At The Guild CN, we specialize in helping entrepreneurs access and navigate the Southern China manufacturing network where 90% of world production takes place.

During your 10-day trip with us, we will bring you to three major cities: Shenzhen, Guangzhou, and Hong Kong, described as the “Silicon Valley of Hardware”. Throughout your trip, you will learn how to navigate the manufacturing and electronics market, visit low-volume and high-value factories, grow your network of potential partners, and attend world-class hardware accelerators and innovation centres.

Explore fastest-growing opportunities in China with the brightest Guilders. Limited seats available, Sign up to learn more.


What's Included?

China Manufacturing 101

Visit Guangzhou, attend a workshop lead by a leading manufacturing consultant about China Manufacturing 101 highlighting the misconceptions of “Made in China”.

Visit Chinese Factories

Within a 3 hour radius, there are over 100,000 factories you can access. From electronic components to 3D printers, you name it and we'll find a factory in China that will produce it.

Hardware & Innovation

Tour the world-class hardware accelerators and innovation centres in the Silicon Valley of Hardware. See how these global startups “disrupt“ the hardware industry by leveraging Shenzhen's ecosystem.

Navigate the Electronics Market

We will bring you to Huaqiangbei electronics market, the heart of China’s electronics industry. By visiting Huaqiangbei, you will have access to massive collection of vendors who are selling everything from imitation iPhones to circuit boards and soldering irons. Yes, almost everything you need for your next gadget.

Networking Opportunities

On this 10-day trip, you will be able to explore the amazing Chinese market with other investors, founders and entrepreneurs. You will also have a unique opportunity to network with the passionate entrepreneurs from the local startup community. You may even find your business partner.
