About Us.

The Guild CN’s mission is to bring more effective and efficient Chinese manufacturing solutions to Western startups and deliver a win-win situation. By presenting you and your company directly to the Chinese accelerators and factories, you will have access to the first-hand information faster and cheaper!

As quoted by Nick Vardy, Co-founder of PinPress, a Waterloo company enrolled in Hax accelerator in 2015 "We came here for mostly research and development of our product, things in China can be done faster and cheaper than in Western or European countries, things that take a week with a large minimum order might be done overnight for half the price for example”.

The Guild CN is designed to be a productive fun trip, bringing the brightest Guilders together to explore building a richer network and new business opportunity. This is trip where we will work hard and play even harder. The Guild is an incredible one-of-a-kind opportunity.

Due to the focus of networking and personalization on this trip, limited seats are available.

Connect & Explore.

At The Guild CN, we specialize in helping entrepreneurs access and navigate the Southern China manufacturing network where 90% of the world's production takes place.

During your 10-day trip with us, we will bring you to three major cities Shenzhen, Guangzhou, and Hong Kong described as the “Silicon Valley of Hardware”. Throughout your trip, you will learn how to navigate the manufacturing and electronics market, visit low-volume and high-value factories, grow your network of potential partners, and attend world-class hardware accelerators and innovation centres.

Explore fastest-growing opportunities in China with the brightest Guilders. Limited seats available, Sign up to learn more.


What's Included?

China Manufacturing 101

Visit Guangzhou, attend a workshop lead by a leading manufacturing consultant about China Manufacturing 101 highlighting the misconceptions of “Made in China”.

Visit Chinese Factories

Within a 3 hour radius, there are over 100,000 factories you can access. From electronic components to 3D printers, you name it and we'll find a factory in China that will produce it.

Hardware & Innovation

Tour the world-class hardware accelerators and innovation centres in the Silicon Valley of Hardware. See how these global startups “disrupt“ the hardware industry by leveraging Shenzhen's ecosystem.

Navigate the Electronics Market

We will bring you to Huaqiangbei electronics market, the heart of China’s electronics industry. By visiting Huaqiangbei, you will have access to a massive collection of vendors who are selling everything from cell phones to circuit boards and soldering irons. Yes, almost everything you need for your next device.

Networking Opportunities

On this 10-day trip, you will be able to explore the amazing Chinese market with other investors, founders and entrepreneurs. You will also have a unique opportunity to network with passionate entrepreneurs from the local startup community. You may even find your business partner.
